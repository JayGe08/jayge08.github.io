🔍 What localization really means

🧭 Scope

  • Keyboard shortcuts & input

⚙️ Process

  • Internationalization (i18n) foundation

🎯 Outcomes

  • Brand consistency across regions
  • Compliance with local laws
  • ✨ Delightful user experience

📖 Translation

Converting text from source to target language. Words → words.

“Hello” → “Hola”

🌍 Localization

Full adaptation: language + format + culture + UX.

“Hello” → “¡Hola! ¿cómo estás?” + € + 24h + local holiday

📌 Example: A US app shows “$10.00” and “April 5, 2026”.
In Germany, it becomes “10,00 €” and “5. April 2026” — and the date format DD.MM.YYYY. In Japan, the currency symbol changes to ¥ and the order of address fields adjusts.
